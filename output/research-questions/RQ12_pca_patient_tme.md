# RQ12: PCA of Patient-Level TME Space

## Status: COMPLETED

## Biological Question
What are the dominant axes of inter-patient TME variation? Do PCA scores predict survival?

## Key Results
- PC1 (24.3%), PC2 (17.0%), PC3 (10.0%); top 5 explain 68% variance
- PC1 = immune/B-cell axis (rho=-0.57 with B-cell clusters, rho=-0.45 with Shannon)
- PC1 does NOT correlate with Gleason (rho=-0.11, p=0.13)
- No PC predicts survival (all Cox p>0.15)
