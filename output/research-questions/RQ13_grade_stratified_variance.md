# RQ13: Grade-Stratified Inter-Patient Heterogeneity

## Status: COMPLETED

## Biological Question
Does high-grade disease show more or less TME heterogeneity than low-grade?

## Key Results
- Overall mean SD vs grade: Spearman rho=-0.90, p=0.037 — CONVERGING
- High-grade patients have less inter-patient TME variance than low-grade
- Levene's test: no single cell type reaches FDR<0.1 (n=10 in GG5 limits power)
