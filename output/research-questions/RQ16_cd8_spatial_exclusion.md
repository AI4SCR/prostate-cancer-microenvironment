# RQ16: CD8+ T-Cell Spatial Exclusion and T-Cell Subtype Balance

## Status: COMPLETED

## Biological Question
Does CD8+ T-cell spatial exclusion from tumor epithelium predict clinical outcomes? What is the immune phenotype landscape (inflamed / excluded / desert)?

## Key Results
- Immune phenotype distribution: Inflamed 72% (343/476 ROIs), Desert 25% (119/476), Excluded 3% (14/476)
- Excluded phenotype is too rare in this cohort for powered analysis
- FoxP3+ Tregs: rbc=0.62 with inflammation (FDR<1e-10); rho=0.22 with Gleason (FDR=0.002)
- FoxP3+ Cox model numerically unstable (near-separation); CD8+ spatial metrics do not predict survival after Gleason adjustment (all p>0.1)
